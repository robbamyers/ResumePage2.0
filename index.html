  • Transfered to Lancaster Catholic High School
  • Discovered my passion for art specifically drawing
  • Graduated from Hempfield High School

  • I purchase my first home

  • After what was planned to be only a year or two stay (which ended up being nearly seven), I sell my home in Coconut Creek, Florida and relocate to my hometown of Lancaster, PA
